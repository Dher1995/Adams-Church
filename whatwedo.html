WHAT WE DO

The block management specialists you’ve been looking for

Whether you’re a resident management company seeking a fully managed service, a self-managed block looking for bespoke options, a director who wants to find the best fire risk assessment out there or a leaseholder curious about right to manage

TALK TO US

TOTAL PEACE OF MIND

Fully Managed

If you’re looking for a positive, forward-thinking managing agent that seriously cares about leaseholders, residents and the buildings you live in then you’ve come to the right place. We’ll be your company secretary, handle all your annual budgets, service charges, credit control and end of year accounts, deal with your regular services, maintenance and major works, ensure you have the strongest health and safety file possible and make insurance a doddle.

FINANCIAL SUCCESS

Speedy Year End Accounts

Taking care of the finances of your block is one of the most important management jobs to do. If you’re a self-managed block you’ve spent the year keeping clear records of all your income and expenditure. Now it’s time to put together a compliant set of accounts ready for filing. That’s where we come in. Our bookkeepers do this day in, day out. They can turn your accounts around in super quick time and they’re a friendly bunch to boot! If it’s a set of end of year accounts you’re looking for, we’re the team you need.

KEEPING YOU SAFE

Health And Safety

Keeping residents safe and making sure blocks are fully compliant is no laughing matter. But we enjoy what we do so handling health and safety is our pleasure. We’ve partnered with some of the best in the business and we’d love to share our panel of approved experts with you. Rely on us to arrange your cladding inspections, fire risk assessments, health and safety reports, asbestos surveys, insurance revaluations and electrical hard-wiring tests.

TAKE BACK CONTROL

Right To Manage

Tired of having zero control over what happens at your block? We’ve helped thousands of leaseholders claim the right to manage. It’s one of our biggest joys to see unethical freeholders and their expensive agents get the boot and once we’re on the case we fight for your right!
