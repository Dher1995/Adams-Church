EXPERT BLOCK MANAGEMENT

The self-management service where everything is at your fingertips

Managing your own block can be a hugely rewarding and cost-saving practice for you and your leaseholders.

But it can also be time-consuming and stressful.

Keeping track of payments, issuing service charge demands, budgeting, planning for major works, dealing with challenging residents, meeting health and safety regulations and completing year end accounts can all make self-management a chore.

We believe in empowering self-managed blocks to achieve great standards and make life easier. Our fully supported industry leading services and products put you firmly in control.

Stress Free Account

Fully compliant end of year accounts professionally drafted by our in-house experts and filed at companies house. Simple.

Protecting Your Property

Fantastically comprehensive buildings and directors insurance at leaseholder-friendly rates.

Safety Made Easy

Cladding certificates, fire risk assessments, asbestos surveys, electrical inspections, insurance revaluations, health and safety risk reports are all just a click away.

Keeping Your Complaint

From registering new directors to filing your PSC registers, all your company secretarial duties are taken care of by our own in-house team.

Money Managed Better

Day to day service charge invoicing, credit control and reporting all made quick and effortless, in one easy-to-manage location.

Sorting Section 20

Fully compliant Section 20 notices and helpful guidance is on hand to take the stress out of managing your major works.

Information is Power

Join our self-management team and be kept informed with regular free industry updates and subscriptions.

Land Registry Access

Download copies of leases, titles, and registers super-fast via our land registry e-services portal.
